Greek American Student Association

In 2023 I re-established the Greek American Student Association (GASA for short) after 5 years of it being inactive at the University of Florida due to Covid. In the short time it has been active we have had many socials, and had the GASA dance troupe perform traditional Greek dances at Europe day. Through this club I have learned leadership skills, and many things about my own culture I previously did not know from creating fun and unique events based around my culture.

Cuban American Student Association

I have created many connections with others in the Cuban American Student Association, connected with my own culture, and learned about the linguistic differences between other hispanic cultures. I have made many professional connections through this club.

Gator Salsa

From a young age I would dance salsa with my dad, and he never learned how to dance casino style. He encouraged me to join Gator Salsa to learn the things he did not so that I may be able to teach him and other friends how to do it. It has been a great experience and allowed me to create a unique collaboration for people at Gator Salsa to learn traditional Greek dancing.

Open Source Club

Open Source Code has encouraged me to be constantly learning more about coding, and sharpening my skills. It promotes camaraderie with open source group coding projects. Aside from coding I have also had the privilege of joining their intramural soccer teams to extend the fun both inside and outside of the classroom.
