Study Abroad

American College of Greece

June 8, 2024 - July 26, 2024

"Παν μέτρον άριστον", the first lesson I received while I was at the American College of Greece: to do everything in moderation. At the college, I was fortunate enough to be a part of their Heritage Greece Program (@heritagegreece on Instagram). This program takes students with Greek ancestral roots from all over the United States and Canada to teach them in Greece about their culture. The activities done throughout the program bring a mix of the touristic highlights as well as more localized aspects of Greece.

Throughout the Heritage Greece Program, we were able to experience that, and so much more. It opened my eyes to how much more there is to do in Greece and what it really is. It also showed me the true Greek values and the Greek way of life. Prior to coming to Heritage Greece I knew that there were some differences with the Greek way of life than the American way of life, but never fully understood it until experiencing it. For example, it is socially acceptable for siestas in the middle of the day, unlike American culture. In addition, I did not know that a good olive oil would burn you if you sipped it like wine to extract the flavor. Another fun fact is that most construction is done in the winter months in Athens so that tourists do not see it.

One thing I especially loved about the program was that we were required to take a Greek language class in the mornings before the activities. A majority of the days, we would end up using the language we learned in class outside of the classroom in everyday situations. In addition, you are paired with students that study at the university and live with you during the duration of the session. If anyone is interested in applying for Heritage Greece I highly recommend it, and you can view some of my experience down below.
